About

Akul Mallayya Swami is a safety-critical embedded systems engineer specializing in FDA-regulated medical devices. His work focuses on integrating embedded firmware, wireless communication, ultra-low-power hardware, and machine learning–based automation into cancer treatment and medical recovery systems to improve regulatory compliance, reliability, and patient safety. He is experienced in standards-aligned verification frameworks under IEC 62304 and ISO 14971, and leads CAPA processes and safety testing in regulated product environments.
